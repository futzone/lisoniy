### 2. Ilmiy Tadqiqotlar va Kontekst Muhandisligi (Research Papers)
Tizimning "miyasi" qanday ishlashi haqidagi fundamental tadqiqotlar:

*   **A Survey of Context Engineering (ArXiv, 2025):** LLMlar uchun kontekst muhandisligi bo‘yicha 1400 dan ortiq maqolani tahlil qilgan keng qamrovli ilmiy ish.
    *   [A Survey of Context Engineering for Large Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.13334)
*   **Lost in the Haystack (PMC):** Katta hajmdagi ma'lumotlar ichida "ignani topish" (Needle-in-a-Haystack) muammosi va yechimlari.
    *   [Lost in the Haystack: Smaller Needles are More Difficult for LLMs to Find](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.18148v1)
*   **Late Chunking (ArXiv):** RAG tizimlarida matnni bo‘laklashning yangi va samarali usuli (Jina AI tomonidan).
    *   [Late Chunking: Contextual Chunk Embeddings](https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.10173)
*   **NoLiMa Benchmark:** Uzun kontekstda modelning mantiqiy fikrlashini tekshirish uchun yangi test tizimi.
    *   [NoLiMa: Long-Context Evaluation Beyond Literal Matching](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.05167)
